How to Use

Welcome to the Object Detection App! This app allows you to perform live object detection using your webcam or upload an image for custom detection. Here's how to use it:

  • Live Detection: Click on "Start Live Detection" to use your webcam for real-time object detection. Click "Stop Live Detection" to stop.
  • Custom Detection: Click on "Start Custom Detection" to upload an image from your device. Click "Detect Objects" after selecting an image.
